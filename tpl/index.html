The traditional presentation deck brings a lot of problems along with it. Viewers have to wait to ask questions or interrupt and disrupt everyone. We think that's wrong, and we aim to change it.

1. Run your presentation

HTML5 presentations are just as flexible and powerful as any big-box slide-deck software, but you can play them anywhere you have a browser, and you need no additional licenses. Web-tech is free.

2. Empower your viewers

Your viewers can ask questions about anything in the presentation without disrupting you or the other viewers. More importantly, they don't have to wait till the end and risk forgetting their question either.

3. Control the conversation

Just because someone asked a question doesn't mean it needs an answer immediately. Sometimes you're "getting to that in a minute" or it's in the next slide. Display any question at any time.

4. Present Better

Not only are your presentations instantly better, but you gain valuable feedback on what you need to add, or things you need to change.
